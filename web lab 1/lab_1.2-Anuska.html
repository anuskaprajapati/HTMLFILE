Department of Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science at Padmakanya Multiple Campus offers a comprehensive program designed to prepare students for careers in the field of computing. Our curriculum is designed to provide both theoreticaland practical knowledge, ensuring that our graduates are well-equipped to meet the demands of the industry.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science & Information Technology
  • Master of Science in Computer Science & Information Technology

Facilities and Resources

We provide state-of-the-art facilities and resources to support our students' learning and research:

  • Modern computer labs equipped with the latest hardware and software
  • Research centers focused on AI, cybersecurity, and data science
  • Collaborative spaces for group projects and study
  • Access to online libraries and databases
